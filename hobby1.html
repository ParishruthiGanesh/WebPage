My Passion for Carnatic Music

I am fascinated by and have completed training in Carnatic vocal music. My love for Indian classical music runs deep, as it is a form of art that connects emotions, traditions, and spirituality.

Carnatic music, with its intricate ragas and talas, has always inspired me. The depth of expression and the discipline it requires have helped me grow both as a musician and as an individual. Singing classical compositions allows me to connect with the cultural roots of India and appreciate the nuances of musical storytelling.
